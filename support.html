Frequently Asked Questions

  • The game crashes or freezes. Force close the app and reopen it. If the issue continues, restart your device and update to the latest app version.
  • I did not receive a purchase. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase, then use Apple's "Restore Purchases" flow in the app.
  • My score or progress is missing. Ensure you are signed in to Game Center and using a stable internet connection before playing.
